The Mission

The mission of Air University is to secure excellence in teaching and research for making graduates with the best professional knowledge, the building of character, a good sense of social responsibility, and a passion for long-term learning. The University shall remain committed to creating an environment conducive for attracting the genius students, faculty, and supporting staff to contribute to the development of a prosperous, peaceful, and enlightened society. Air University pursues to stand among the leading national universities, succeeding in teaching, learning, research, innovation, and public service.
